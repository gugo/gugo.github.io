BALLOOPS

A Project by:

Tommaso Cappelletti: Videomaker / Near Future Designer

Guglielmo Torelli: Visual Designer / 3D Artist

Balloops is a Visual Design created for be projected at "Villa La Magia", Quarrata in occasion of a food and craft fair. During this event there was a Balloon art exhibition for the kids, so we created an environment to make them play.
