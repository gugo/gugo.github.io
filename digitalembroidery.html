Fedele Nacucchi: Engineer / Maker

Guglielmo Torelli: Designer / Maker / Programmer

Digital Embroidery is an art installation developed during XYLab and exhibited at Kastrign√†na Etnographic Museum of Past and Future. Starting from an etnographic research on the local people of a town in the very south of Italy, called Castrignano de' Greci, our team developed a concept based on the human craftsmanship behaviour with the aim of translating the old artisan skills in our future skills. Starting from the old ladies embroidery abilities we developed a video installation in which we project ourselves in the future doing what they are doing now but in a different way.
